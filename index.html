About Us

Henaku Farm’s is one of West Africa’s leading firms in the agribusiness, with over 2 decades of experience operating in the Volta Region of Ghana, we have established ourselves as one the most reliable farms in the country. We offer a wide range of products that we distribute throughout the country to various consumers, ranging for exotic fruits such as mangos and plantain to more wholesale goods such in wheat and grains, the farm can cater and provide to any customer’s needs.
